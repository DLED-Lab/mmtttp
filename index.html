Objectives of FDP

About DLED Lab

At the Prof Dhananjay Lab for Education Design (DLED), we’re pushing the boundaries of educational technology and creative learning. Established through a unique partnership between the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Ropar and ExcelSoft Technologies Private Limited, our lab is a hub for cutting-edge research and innovation.

Our Mission: Combine academic rigor with industry expertise to create impactful solutions for social good. Foster a collaborative environment where brilliant minds explore, experiment, and generate groundbreaking ideas in educational technology.

Lab Projects

CAL (Continuous Active Learning)

CAL focuses on real-time engagement and adaptive learning strategies to ensure continuous student involvement. It leverages AI-driven feedback loops to personalize content and pace.

PES (Peer Evaluation System)

PES is designed to enhance peer feedback in educational contexts. It streamlines the evaluation process, encourages constructive critiques, and fosters a collaborative learning environment.

DDD (Dopamine Driving through Dashboard)

DDD uses interactive dashboards to track and enhance student motivation by leveraging real-time data and analytics. It’s designed to provide timely feedback and keep learners engaged throughout their academic journey.
